About Me

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina in February of 1989. My parents bought me my first computer when I was 8 years old, and, a few years later, I put it to good use and built my first website using all the power of Microsoft FrontPage 98.

In 2008, I got my first job as a "Dreamweaver Programmer". That was the actual job title. I had a thing for WYSIWYG editors apparently. I spent the first few years of my career working as a PHP/SQL Developer, until 2011 when I switched to a front-end role and I joined Globant to work as a UI Developer. I've been in a love-hate relationship with JavaScript ever since.

In 2015, I joined the amazing Help Scout team, where I currently work as a Senior Engineer, specializing in third-party JavaScript applications. I live and work (remotely) from the beautiful city of Los Angeles, CA with my wife and best friend Patricia, our daughters Julia and Sofia, and our dogs Dulce and Eddie.
